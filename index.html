How to eat in a corean table?

The best part of learning about new cuisines is trying delicious dishes and ingredients that are entirely new to you. And an important element of any cuisine is the customs and etiquette that govern how meals are eaten.

Gastronomic courses and workshops

At KOREAN HUB the workshops are dynamic and participatory. Attendees fully prepare recipes with organic and korean products, with the idea of ​​promoting a quality, healthy and sustainable diet. At the end of the workshop, the dishes are shared in an informal and relaxed atmosphere.
